# Privacy Policy — Quote Drop

**Last updated: June 23, 2026**

This Privacy Policy describes how Quote Drop handles your information. We built Quote Drop to be a private, minimal app — it does not collect personal data, run advertising, or share anything with third parties.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

**We do not collect any personal information.**

The App has no accounts, no sign-up, and no server of its own. Everything you do in the App stays on your device or in your personal iCloud account.

Specifically, the App stores the following **on your device only**:

| Data | Purpose |
|---|---|
| Your saved favorites | So you can revisit quotes you love |
| Your personal notes on quotes | Private annotations you write |
| Your notification preferences (on/off, time) | To send your daily reminder |
| Your selected theme | To remember your appearance preference |
| Your daily scroll count | To enforce free-tier limits, reset daily |

None of this data is transmitted to us or any third party.

---

## 2. iCloud Sync

If you are signed into iCloud on your device, the App will sync your **favorites and notes** to your personal iCloud Key-Value Store. This sync happens directly between your device and Apple's iCloud servers — we never see, access, or store this data.

This means:
- Your favorites and notes follow you if you switch devices or reinstall the App.
- The data is governed by [Apple's iCloud Terms and Conditions](https://www.apple.com/legal/internet-services/icloud/).
- You can disable iCloud sync at any time in **iOS Settings → [Your Name] → iCloud → Quote Drop**.

---

## 3. Subscriptions and Purchases

Premium subscriptions are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We never see or store your payment details, billing information, or Apple ID.

Your subscription status is verified directly with Apple via StoreKit on your device. Apple's handling of purchase data is governed by [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/).

---

## 4. Notifications

Quote Drop sends **local notifications** only — daily reminders that are scheduled and triggered entirely on your device. No notification data is sent to a server. We have no way to track whether you open or dismiss a notification.

You can enable, disable, or reschedule notifications at any time in **Quote Drop → Settings → Daily Reminder**, or in **iOS Settings → Notifications → Quote Drop**.

---

## 5. Analytics and Tracking

The App contains **no analytics, crash reporting, advertising SDKs, or tracking of any kind**. We do not use any third-party services that could collect data about your behavior.

---

## 6. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. The App contains no features that would require children to submit personal data.

---

## 7. Data Retention and Deletion

Because we collect no data on our end, there is nothing for us to delete. All data (favorites, notes, preferences) lives on your device or in your iCloud account.

To delete everything:
- **Delete the App** — removes all locally stored data from your device.
- **Disable iCloud sync** (see Section 2) and delete the App — removes iCloud-synced data as well.

---

## 8. Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy in a meaningful way — for example, if a future version of the App adds a new feature that handles data differently — we will update the "Last updated" date above and post the new policy at the same URL. Continued use of the App after a change constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
